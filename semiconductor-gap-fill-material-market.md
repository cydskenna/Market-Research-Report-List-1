<p><p>The semiconductor gap fill material market encompasses critical applications such as Shallow Trench Isolation (STI), Inter-Metal Dielectric (IMD), and Pre-Metal Dielectric (PMD). STI is used to electrically isolate transistors, enhancing device performance. IMD materials fill gaps between metal layers, improving interconnect reliability and signal integrity. PMD serves as an insulating layer before metal deposition, ensuring optimal performance and reducing cross-talk. These materials are essential for advancing semiconductor technology and enabling smaller, more efficient electronic devices.</p></p>
