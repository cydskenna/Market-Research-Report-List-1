<p><p>The tire and rubber lubricants market is categorized into natural and synthetic types. Synthetic lubricants, on the other hand, are chemically engineered, providing superior performance, thermal stability, and better resistance to oxidation. They enhance the efficiency of tire production processes and improve the longevity and performance of rubber products, making them popular in the industry.</p></p>
