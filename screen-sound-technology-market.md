<p><ul><li>Cantilever Piezoelectric Ceramic Sound</li><li>Screen Exciter Sound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screen sound technology encompasses various methods to enhance audio in display systems. The cantilever piezoelectric ceramic sound type utilizes piezoelectric materials to generate sound waves from vibrations, delivering clear audio without bulky speakers. Conversely, the screen exciter sound market employs transducer technology that converts the display surface into a sound source, providing immersive audio experiences without additional hardware. Both types leverage innovative approaches to merge sound with visual displays, improving user experience in various applications, including televisions and mobile devices.</p></p>
