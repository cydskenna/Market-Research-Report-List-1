<p><ul><li>Short Wavelength Infrared Thermal Cameras</li><li>Mid-Wavelength Infrared Thermal Cameras</li><li>Long-Wavelength Infrared Thermal Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rugged thermal cameras market is segmented into three types based on wavelength: short, mid, and long-wavelength infrared thermal cameras. Short-wavelength infrared cameras operate between 0.9 to 1.7 microns, ideal for detecting moisture and surface temperature variations. Mid-wavelength infrared cameras, spanning 3 to 5 microns, are commonly used for industrial applications, offering high sensitivity to temperature changes. Long-wavelength infrared cameras, ranging from 8 to 14 microns, excel in detecting heat signatures, making them suitable for surveillance and firefighting scenarios.</p></p>
