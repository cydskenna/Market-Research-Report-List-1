<p><p>Styreneic block copolymers (SBCs) consist primarily of SEBS (styrene-ethylene-butylene-styrene) and SIS (styrene-isoprene-styrene) types, among others. SEBS, known for its remarkable thermal stability and elasticity, is primarily used in applications requiring toughness and flexibility, like adhesives and automotive components. SIS provides excellent adhesion and is commonly found in sealants and coatings. Other SBC types include SIBS (styrene-isobutylene-styrene) and SEPS (styrene-ethylene-propylene-styrene), each serving specialized markets in packaging, medical devices, and consumer goods due to their unique properties.</p></p>
