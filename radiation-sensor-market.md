<p><p>The radiation sensor market includes various types, notably the CSP (Ceramic Sensor Package) and CerPin (Ceramic Pin) technologies. CSP utilizes a ceramic housing that enhances durability and stability in radiation detection, ensuring reliable performance in harsh environments. CerPin sensors, on the other hand, offer a compact design with pins for easy integration into electronic systems, making them ideal for portable devices. Both types cater to diverse applications, including medical imaging, nuclear safety, and environmental monitoring, facilitating effective radiation management.</p></p>
