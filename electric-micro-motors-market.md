<p><p>Electric micro-motors can be categorized into two primary types: AC and DC motors. AC micro-motors operate on alternating current and are typically used in applications requiring consistent speed and efficiency, such as in household appliances and industrial machines. On the other hand, DC micro-motors function on direct current, offering higher torque at lower speeds, making them ideal for portable devices, robotics, and automotive applications. Each type has distinct advantages depending on the specific application requirements and operational needs.</p></p>
