<p><p>The booklet label market encompasses various types, including pressure-sensitive labels, glue-applied labels, and others. Pressure-sensitive labels are adhesive-backed, allowing for easy application without heat or water, ideal for quick labeling. Glue-applied labels require adhesive application during production, often providing a more permanent attachment to the substrate. Other types may include wet-glue labels, shrink sleeves, and linerless labels, each tailored for specific packaging needs or product requirements, enhancing product presentation and information delivery.</p></p>
