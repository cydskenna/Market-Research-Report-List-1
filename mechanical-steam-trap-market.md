<p><ul><li>Float type (Lever Float and Free Float)</li><li>Inverted Bucket type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical steam trap market comprises various types, including float type and inverted bucket type traps. Float type traps, such as lever float and free float, utilize a floating mechanism to control steam and condensate flow, ensuring efficient operation and minimal steam loss. Inverted bucket traps, on the other hand, feature a buoyant bucket that closes off steam when filled with condensate, allowing for reliable separation of steam and water. Both types offer distinct advantages in industrial applications, emphasizing efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
