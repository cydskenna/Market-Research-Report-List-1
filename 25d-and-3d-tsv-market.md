<p><p>2.5D and 3D Through-Silicon Vias (TSV) are advanced packaging technologies used in semiconductor design to enhance performance and reduce footprint. 2.5D TSV involves stacking multiple chips on a silicon interposer, facilitating high-density interconnections between them. In contrast, 3D TSV technology allows for vertical stacking of multiple dies within a single package, enabling even tighter integration and improved data transfer rates.</p><p>The 2.5D and 3D TSV Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.6% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing demand for high-performance computing, memory, and mobile applications, particularly in data centers and artificial intelligence. The proliferation of IoT devices and the need for efficient data processing and storage are also fueling market expansion.</p><p>Latest trends indicate a shift towards heterogeneous integration, where different types of chips are combined within a single package to improve functionality and performance. Additionally, advancements in manufacturing techniques and materials are enhancing the reliability and scalability of TSV technologies. The trend towards miniaturization and energy efficiency will likely continue to propel the adoption of 2.5D and 3D TSV solutions in various industries.</p></p>
