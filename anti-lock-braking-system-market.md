<p><p>The Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) market is primarily divided into two types: Pneumatic ABS and Hydraulic ABS. Pneumatic Anti-Lock Braking Systems utilize compressed air to manage brake pressure, commonly found in commercial vehicles and larger automobiles. In contrast, Hydraulic Anti-Lock Braking Systems utilize fluid dynamics to optimize brake performance, widely adopted in passenger cars and motorcycles. Both systems enhance vehicle safety by preventing wheel lock-up during braking, thereby improving control and reducing stopping distances in various driving conditions.</p></p>
