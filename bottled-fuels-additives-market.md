<p><p>Bottled fuel additives enhance fuel performance and longevity. Corrosion inhibitors protect engines from metal deterioration, while demulsifiers prevent water contamination in fuels. Cetane improvers boost ignition quality in diesel engines, and detergents keep injectors clean. Deactivators neutralize harmful substances in fuel, while antioxidants prevent oxidation and fuel degradation. Octane improvers enhance gasoline performance, increasing efficiency. Other additives may include stabilizers or lubricants, collectively serving to optimize engine performance, reduce emissions, and extend the lifespan of fuels and engines.</p></p>
